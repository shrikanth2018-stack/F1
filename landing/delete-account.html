Delete your 1stOne account

You can request deletion of your 1stOne account and your associated data at any time. We process every request within 7 business days.

How to request deletion

Email support@1stone.in from any address with the subject line Delete my account, and include in the body:

  • The phone number registered on your 1stOne account (E.164 format, e.g., +919448364017)
  • Optionally, a brief reason — this helps us improve the service

We reply within 24 hours to confirm receipt. Deletion is completed within 7 business days, and you receive a final confirmation by email.

What gets deleted

On deletion, the following data is permanently removed from our active systems:

  • Your name, phone number, and any email address on file
  • All saved delivery addresses and location pins
  • Your wallet balance (refunded — see below) and transaction history
  • Your subscription history and any saved preferences
  • Your push notification tokens (so you stop receiving messages)
  • Per-item ratings and feedback you've submitted
  • Your device's session and login records
What we keep (and why)

For legal and accounting compliance, certain order-level records must be retained even after account deletion. These records are anonymised — your name and phone number are removed — but the underlying transaction is kept:

  • Order invoices and tax records — retained for 8 years under Indian Income Tax Act and GST law
  • Payment transaction logs — retained for 7 years per Razorpay's PCI-DSS and RBI compliance obligations
  • Audit trails for fraud prevention — retained for up to 3 years

None of these retained records can be linked back to you personally after account deletion.

Wallet refund

If your wallet has a positive balance at the time of deletion, we refund the balance to your original Razorpay payment method (5–7 business days) or, if that's not possible, to a bank account or UPI ID you confirm by email. There's no fee or deduction.

Active subscriptions

If you have an active subscription, please pause or cancel it before requesting account deletion. Otherwise we'll cancel it on your behalf during the deletion process and pro-rata refund unused days to your wallet, then refund the wallet as described above.

Pending orders

Any orders still in transit are completed — we won't cancel a meal that's already cooked or already on its way. Once the order is delivered (or cancelled in normal flow), the deletion process continues.

Re-registering later

You're welcome to re-register at any time using the same phone number. However, the new account will not retain any data from the deleted one — it starts fresh, including a new wallet balance and no past order history.
