How it works

Open the 1stOne app and browse the day's fresh menu or choose a subscription plan. Pick your delivery cycle — breakfast, lunch, snacks, or dinner — and pay via UPI or wallet. Our team prepares your meal in our home kitchen the same morning and a local rider hand-delivers it warm to your door. No middlemen. No factory food. Just home cooking, scaled.

Subscription plans

Subscribe once and forget about daily ordering. Choose a 7-day, 15-day, or 30-day plan and lock in your meals at the best price. Pause any day with one tap, skip cycles when you travel, resume whenever. All plans include the same fresh menu — only the duration changes. Wallet credits roll over, so unused days never go to waste.

Coverage area

We currently deliver across Siddapur and surrounding zones in Uttara Kannada district, Karnataka. Our coverage expands as more home cooks join the network. Enter your address in the app to check live availability — if you're outside our base zone, we may still deliver via a partner hub.
